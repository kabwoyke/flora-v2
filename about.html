Our Story

Your Trusted Real Estate Partner in Kenya

Flora Prime Properties was founded with a single vision: to make land ownership accessible, transparent, and stress-free for every Kenyan. Led by Director Paul K. Ngugi, our firm has grown from a small consultancy into one of Nairobi's respected real estate agencies.

We specialise in premium plots, residential homes, and agricultural land across Kenya — from Karen and Runda to the scenic highlands of Limuru and Nanyuki. Every property we list is verified for legal compliance before it reaches our clients.
