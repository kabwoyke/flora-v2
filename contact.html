Frequently Asked

Common Questions

How do I verify a property's title deed?

+
All properties listed by Flora Prime have already undergone title verification through the Ministry of Lands. We share the search certificate with buyers before any deposit is made. You can also independently conduct a title search at any Lands Registry office across Kenya.

Do you offer payment plans or instalment options?

+
Yes. For qualifying properties, we offer structured instalment plans of 6, 12, or 24 months with a deposit. Contact our agents for the specific terms that apply to a property you are interested in.

What fees should I expect when buying property?

+
Typical transaction costs include stamp duty (2–4% of value), legal fees, and transfer fees. Flora Prime's agents provide a full cost breakdown before you commit, so there are no unexpected costs.

Can I buy property in Kenya if I live abroad?

+
Absolutely. We work with diaspora clients regularly. Transactions can be conducted remotely via power of attorney, and we facilitate virtual site visits and video walkthroughs. Reach out via WhatsApp or email to begin.

How long does the sale process typically take?

+
A typical property sale takes 30–90 days from offer acceptance to title transfer, depending on the property type and Lands Registry timelines. Our legal team actively follows up to keep things on track.
